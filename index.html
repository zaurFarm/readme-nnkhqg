The Most Popular New Rolex Watches of 2026 So Far

Watches and Wonders 2026 delivered one of the strongest Rolex release years in recent memory. Six months in, the market has sorted the winners from the merely interesting. Here are the models generating the most demand, ranked by a combination of grey market premium, AD allocation difficulty, and verified sales volume.

The top five

  • 100th Anniversary Oyster 36mm — premiums of 40 percent and climbing
  • Green Ombré Datejust 36 on Jubilee — hardest single reference to secure at AD
  • Steel Daytona with Opaline Frost subdials — consistent 90 to 100 percent premium
  • Jubilee Gold Day-Date 36 — first-year proprietary alloy driving collector urgency
  • Turquoise Datejust 41 on Jubilee — the breakout colorway of the year

What the premiums tell us

When five different references all command premiums above thirty percent, it signals broad-based demand rather than speculation on a single model. Rolex managed to create desire across multiple price points and complications — from the entry-level Oyster Perpetual to the gold Day-Date. That breadth is unusual and suggests 2026 production is being absorbed faster than any recent year.
